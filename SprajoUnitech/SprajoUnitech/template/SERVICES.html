Refrigeration Services


               Refrigeration services and solutions that can help to keep your system up and running year-round.Our satisfied customers include cold storage warehouses, food processing plants, chemical plants, and many other industries. We take pride in all our work and are dedicated to satisfying our customer's needs. We design, engineer, install, and maintain refrigeration systems that use many types of equipment and develop a wide range of temperatures. We have highly trained refrigeration technicians with a good knowledge and experience. Our equipment includes top of the line screw compressors, reciprocating compressors, evaporative condensers, air units, hygienic air handlers, glycol and water chillers, blast freezers, and computerized controls. We can design and install a system that will maintain any temperature. We also have continuous training programs to keep our refrigeration technicians up-to-date with the latest trends in the industry.


           Industrial Refrigeration Service has the finest engineers in the industry. Many of our engineers have served on committees, striving to better the refrigeration industry. We can turn your refrigeration ideas into a reality that will be cost effective and dependable. Years of experience, expert load calculations, as well as equipment requirements are all combined to ensure the system will work to your specifications.

           Industrial Refrigeration Service utilizes highly trained pipe fitters and certified pipe welders. These experienced professionals have fabricated numerous refrigeration systems and take great pride in providing superior workmanship while meeting or exceeding all refrigeration codes and standards. Materials procured for fabrication are of the highest quality to assure you have a long lasting dependable system.

           Industrial Refrigeration Service has a state-of-the-art 24,000 square foot fabrication shop outfitted with all the essential tools. We have Overhead Cranes, Iron Workers and Welding Machines in one location which affords us the ability to fabricate everything our customers need quickly and efficiently all in one place. Industrial Refrigeration Service can tailor all piping requirements to accommodate the Refrigeration, Power, Gas and Food industries, just to name a few. We can fabricate it all from Structural Steel, Valve Stations, Custom Packaged Piping Skids, to complete Refrigeration Systems.

           Industrial Refrigeration Service employs expert Service Technicians with years of experience in the refrigeration industry. All of our Technicians are knowledgeable in electrical, mechanical, and computer controls. servicing includes cleaning,Gas charging, vacuuming ,brazing etc. We offer 24 hour emergency service along with inspection and maintenance programs designed specifically for each customer, mechanical integrity inspections, commissioning of new equipment, compressor and pump rebuilds, control, trouble shooting and emergency shutdowns, to name a few.


We serve our customers right!
